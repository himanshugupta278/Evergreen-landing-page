Welcome to the Evergreen Web Page

Evergreen calls on 240 ports worldwide in about 80 countries,
and is the seventh-largest company in the shipping industry. Its principal trading routes are East Asia to North America,
Central America and the Caribbean; East Asia to the Mediterranean and Northern Europe;
Europe to the east coast of North America;
East Asia to Australia; East Asia to eastern and southern Africa; East Asia to South America;
and an intra Asia service linking ports in East Asia to the Persian Gulf and the Red Sea
